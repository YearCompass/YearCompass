What is year-planning?

Planning your year is a good habit. It helps you identify your successes and sorrows and will help you realize how much can happen just in a year. By learning from the past you can plan your future so that you don't repeat the same patterns and can feel in control of your own life.

Who created this year-planning workbook?

Volunteers of the Invisible University International.

What is Invisible University International?

Invisible University is an international organization consisting of university students and young adults. Its vision is for every higher educational organization to have a community of volunteers organizing lifestyle-related trainings and events for the rest of the students. Here you can learn more.

How much does the year-planning workbook cost?

A share. Invisible University is able to reach many students with the persistent work of its volunteers and with the help of donations. If you can afford to donate, click the button.

Is there an offline meeting too?

Yes, at the beginning of each year we hold a meetup-like year-planning workshop, as our experience is that meeting in person facilitates things greatly. We encourage you to host an event, invite a few people over, and fill in the workbook together. We can promise you that it will be time well spent. We look forward hearing about your experience in the comments section at the bottom of this page.
